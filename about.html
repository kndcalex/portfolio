Who I Am

My name is John Kendrick Alexander Cabunilas, and I am currently a student with a strong passion for technology and information systems. I have always been fascinated by how computers and software work, which led me to pursue a path in IT. Beyond the screen, I love to travel and explore new places — there is nothing quite like the feeling of discovering somewhere new. I capture the beauty of nature through my camera, freezing sunsets, mountains, and quiet moments into photographs that tell a story. I also express myself through poetry, turning emotions and experiences into words. I believe that creativity and technology go hand in hand, and both fuel my drive to keep growing.

My Education

I am currently pursuing my studies while actively building hands-on experience through various projects and certifications. My coursework has introduced me to programming, web development, and database management. I am committed to applying the theoretical knowledge I gain in class to real-world scenarios through practical projects and self-study.

My Professional Goals

I wanna be an IT professional who can build reliable and efficient digital solutions for businesses and communities. My ultimate goal is to become a web developer capable of creating complete web applications from scratch. I aspire to work in a dynamic tech company where I can contribute my skills while continuing to grow as a developer. In the long term, I hope to use my expertise to help organizations improve their digital presence and operational efficiency — and maybe one day, build a platform where I can share my travels, photography, and poetry with the world.
